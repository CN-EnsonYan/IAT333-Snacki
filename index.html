Snacki Smart Pantry

Discover the Snacki Smart Pantry, Metro Vancouver’s Premier State of The Art 24/7 Unattended Grab & Go Retail Solution.

Replacing traditional vending machines, the Snacki Smart Pantry delivers fresh meals, snacks, and everyday essentials anytime, anywhere.

The Snacki Smart Pantry uses advanced AI computer vision to automatically track selections. With no buttons, no waiting, and no stuck items, the Snacki Smart Pantry offers a seamless grab & go experience just like reaching into your own fridge.

Snacki Machine

Redefining the Metro Vancouver’s vending machine experience with eye-catching, playful, and adorable vending machines.

Snacki Machines offer a rotating selection of imported Asian snacks and drinks through uniquely designed, adorable mascot-driven vending machines.
